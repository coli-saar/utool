Provides codecs for dealing with dominance constraints and dominance graphs. The following codecs are currently defined:
  • domcon-oz (input, output): dominance constraints represented as Mozart/Oz terms, as in the old CHORUS demo;
  • domcon-gxl (input, output): dominance graphs in the GXL graph reprsentation format;
  • domcon-udraw (output): dominance graphs in the concrete syntax of the uDraw(Graph) graph drawing system.
